# <span id="page-3-0"></span>**Description and purpose of functionality**

This instruction describes a user interface of the user account of the Partners and Integrators Register, its functionality, as well as key issues arising in the work with the Partners and Integrator Register in practice.

# <span id="page-4-0"></span>**Getting Started**

To get started, proceed as follows:

• follow the link <https://registry.intuot.crpt.ru>to the Partners and Integrators Register;

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_4_Picture_3.jpeg)

*Figure 1. Login page of the Partners and Integrators Register*

- click the **[ Register ]** button, fill in and send a company registration form for verification (see section "[Creation of registration request"](#page-6-0));
- after approval of the request by the Operator, it is necessary to receive login credentials in a letter sent to the e-mail specified in the request;

If the request is rejected, a notification will be sent to the e-mail with a reason for request rejection. To eliminate the reason specified, it will be necessary to re-submit a registration request with providing correct information.

- log in to the system, familiarize yourself and accept terms of use (see section ["Authorization and](#page-16-0) [access recovery"](#page-16-0));
- check and, if necessary, edit data of the organization profile (see sections ["Editing organization](#page-20-0) [data](#page-20-0)");
- perform actions required to switch an organization profile to the **"Active"** status.

Depending on the organization type assigned to the organization (for more details, see section "[Table of correspondence of organization types to activity types](#page-11-0)"), there are the following scenarios of transition of the profile to the **"Active"** status:

- for a **"Goods circulation participant"** organization type, the **"Active"** status will be provided automatically;
- for **"Partner"**, **"Integrator"**, **"Self-employed developer"**, **"Service provider"** organization types, a status will be changed only after organization is checked by the Security Service.

If the request has been rejected by the Security Service, a corresponding notification will be sent

to the e-mail. In case of any questions, contact the technical support at the address specified in the notification.

- for **"Integrator"**, **"Self-employed developer"** and **"Goods circulation participant"** organization types, add products ("[Management of integration and partner products"](#page-25-0)) and sign related documents. During the transition of the organization profile to **"Active"** status, a list of the documents will be sent to the email (for more information, see section "[Table of correspondence](#page-92-0) [between document mandatory and organization types](#page-92-0)");
- for the **"Service provider"** organization type, fill in a Service Provider questionnaire (see section "[Filling out a questionnaire to connect a Service Provider](#page-99-0)"), wait for transition of the questionnaire to **"Confirmed"** status after the data are checked by the Operator, create and sign an application for Soft and Hardware Package Contract (see section "[Submission of application for Soft and](#page-101-0) [Hardware Package Contract"](#page-101-0)), as well as Soft and Hardware Package Contract generated automatically. Once the documents have been successfully processed, an organization with the **"Service provider"** type will be added to a list of the service providers in the OMS user interface;
- a feature of export of the statistics on FDO and EDM is available for the organizations with **"Fiscal data operator"** and **"Electronic document management operator"** activity types (for more details, see section "Statistics on FDO and EDM").

For more information on the main functionality of the user account of the Partners and Integrators Register, see section ["Major features of the Partners and Integrators Register"](#page-19-0).

# <span id="page-6-0"></span>**Creation of registration request**

To register in the Partners and Integrators Register, follow the link [https://registry.intuot.crpt.ru,](https://registry.intuot.crpt.ru) click the **[ Register ]** button and fill in the fields of the company registration form.

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_6_Picture_2.jpeg)

*Figure 2. Login page*

Instruction on how to work in the Partners and Integrators Register can be downloaded from the following link **"Download instruction on how to register and work in the register"**.

The company registration form shall contain the following information:

• **"Country of business registration"** (by default, **"Russian Federation"**);

If you select a **"Natural person"** value in the **"Business registration form"** field, only **"Russian Federation"** value will be available.

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_7_Picture_0.jpeg)

*Figure 3. Company registration form*

• **Main language** (**"Russian"** is set by default)

Possible values:

- **"Russian"**;
- **"English"**.

**"English"** will be automatically set when a country other than **"Russian Federation"** is selected (but you can still select **"Russian"**).

• **"Form of business registration"** (set the corresponding switch in the (on) mode). Depending on the selected value, a field composition of the company registration form changes.

The selected value is immutable and cannot be edited during further work in the system.

Values:

◦ **"Legal entity"** — legal entity.

When considering a request, the Operator verifies that a legal entity is registered on the territory of the country specified in the **"Country of business registration"** field.

◦ **"IE"** — individual entrepreneur.

Individual entrepreneurs whose type of citizenship differs from "Russian Federation" are allowed to register, provided that the country of business registration for such individual entrepreneurs is the Russian Federation.

◦ **"Natural person"** — natural person. The value shall be selected if a natural person specified is a marking software developer and is registered as a self-employed taxpayer.

This value corresponds to the **"Self-employed developer"** organization type and includes the following possible roles for natural persons:

- integrator of software and hardware solutions;
- developer of cash register software;
- developer of goods accounting software;
- developer of process and resource planning system (ERP system);
- developer of encryption software and hardware and information protection means;
- developer of Track&Trace Software;
- consulting activities;
- software development for service providers.

If you select a **"Natural person"** value, only **"Russian Federation"** value will be available in the **"Country of business registration"** field.

If you select a **"Natural person"** value, a **"Self-employed developer"** value will be automatically activated in the **"3. Activity type"** field and all other values will be blocked. At the same time a **[ Download a consent to personal data processing ]** button will appear in the lower part of the form. Clicking the button allows to download a "Consent to personal data processing" file, which is automatically filled out in line with the request data.

• **"Goods circulation participant"** (unavailable for a **"Natural person"** business registration form). Select a **"Yes"** / **"No"** value by moving the corresponding switch in the (on) mode.

Select **"Yes"** if a company is registered in the TT GIS or MDLP FGIS. If you select a **"Yes"** value, a **"GCP"** value will be automatically activated in the **"3. Activity type"** field and all other values will be blocked.

Select **"No"** in other cases, including if a company interacts with the TT GIS and MDLP FGIS via API, but participation in circulation of the marked goods is limited to the following cases:

- delivery of goods to consumers;
- provision of postal services for COD postal items in the sale of goods by sample or distance sale;
- a company acts as a paying agent, which does not perform circulation of the marked goods;

- purchase or sale of the marked products on the deal, the details of which are a state secret.
- **"Service provider"** (select a **"Yes"** / **"No"** value by moving the corresponding switch in the (on) mode).

**"Yes"** value is only available for the companies with a **"Legal entity"** registration form. When hovering over the information icon , a list of requirements required for companies to be registered as a Service provider becomes available.

Select **"Yes"**, if a company is an active or future Service provider with **"Printing shop"** activity type.

If you select **"Yes"** value, the **"printing shop"** value will be automatically activated in the **"3. Activity type"** block (all other values will be blocked) and a **"Distribution provider"** field will be displayed in which it will be necessary to select a value from the drop-down list.

- by means of direct input in block **"1. Contact person"**, fill in the data of the primary contact who will be registered in the Partners and Integrators Register as administrator and main contact person:
  - **"Full name"** specify last name, first name and patronymic of the contact person;
  - **"E-mail"** specify an e-mail address to be used as login to sign in to the system, as well as to be used for sending a login password and Operator's instructions;
  - **"Phone number"** (numbers only).

A mask **"+7"** is used for this field when selecting a **"Russian Federation"** value in the **"Country of business registration"** field. The mask is not used for other countries.

- **"Position"**.
- in a block **"2. Company details"** (unavailable for a **"Natural person"** business registration form) fill in by means of direct input the following fields: (when considering a request, the input data are checked against EGRUL and EGRIP data):
  - **"Name"** (unavailable if the **"Russian Federation"** value is specified in the **"Country of business registration"** field;
  - **"INN"**.

If the **"Russian Federation"** value is specified in the **"Country of business registration"** field, a length of the value in **"INN"** field will be verified: for legal entities - 10 characters, for individual entrepreneurs - 12 characters.

In case of repeated submission of the registration request previously rejected by the Operator, when entering a value in **"INN"** field, a switch **"Submit a repeat registration request"** will be activated above the **"Name"** field.

- **"KPP"** (unavailable if the **"Russian Federation"** value is specified in the **"Country of business registration"** field;
- **"Main email"** main e-mail address of the company. It may differ from the value specified in **"E-mail"** field in block **"1. Contact person"**;
- **"Phone"** (numbers only). Specify a main phone number of the company. The parameter is optional if a business registration form is **"IE"**.

A mask **"+7"** is used for this field when selecting a **"Russian Federation"** value in the **"Country of business registration"** field. The mask is not used for other countries.

- **"Website"** company website (mandatory field);
- **"Main address"** registration address (it displays only for **"IE"** business registration form);
- **"Company profile"**.

Fill out a description of the company's activities in relation to the TT GIS and MDLP FGIS.

The minimum number of characters is 100.

- in block **"2. Natural person data"** (available only for a **"Natural person"** business registration form) fill in by means of direct input the following fields:
  - **"Full name"** specify last name, first name and patronymic of the natural person;
  - **"INN"** specify 12-digit INN of the natural person;
  - **"Date of birth"** specify a date in "DD.MM.YYYY" format or select a value in the calendar (icon );
  - **"Place of birth"** specify a place of birth (the same as in the passport);
  - **"Registration address"** specify address of the registration of the place of residence;
  - in the **"Passport"** section, specify passport details in the following fields: **"Series"**, **"Number"**, **"Issuing authority"**, **"Issue date"**;
  - **"Main email"** main e-mail address of the natural person. It may differ from the value specified in **"E-mail"** field in block **"1. Contact person"**;
  - **"Phone"** (numbers only). A mask **"+7"** is used for this field;
  - **"Website"** company website (optional field);
  - **"Company profile"**.

Fill out a description of the company's activities in relation to the TT GIS and MDLP FGIS.

The minimum number of characters is 100.

• in the block **"3.Activity type"**, select the relevant values by moving the corresponding switches in the (on) mode.

A choice of the values determines a range of possibilities and scenarios of work in the system available to the registered user of the Partners and Integrators Register. Each activity type corresponds to one of the organization types (functional group): **"Goods circulation participant"**, **"Integrator"**, **"Partner"**, **"Service provider"** or **"Self-employed developer"**. For more information on restrictions and peculiarities of the selection of the activity types, see section "[Table](#page-11-0) [of correspondence of organization types to activity types](#page-11-0)".

A list of the documents to activate features corresponding to the organization types can be found in the section ["Table of correspondence between document mandatory and organization types"](#page-92-0).

A list of activity types can be edited after registration of the organization in the Partners and Integrators Register by submitting a request in the User account (for more details, see section "[Submission of request to change a list of activity types"](#page-24-0)).

### <span id="page-14-0"></span>**Sending of a consent to personal data processing during natural person registration**

If you select a **"Natural person"** value in the **"Form of business registration"** field, a **"Selfemployed developer"** value will be automatically activated in the field of selection of activity types and a **[ Download a consent to personal data processing ]** button will appear in the lower part of the registration form.

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_15_Figure_0.jpeg)

*Figure 4. A button to download a personal data processing consent*

Clicking the button will perform automatic check of the data format in the request. After the check is successfully completed, the download of the file "Consent to personal data processing" filled out on the basis of the entered information is started, and the **[ Choose file ]** button appears to access the upload form.

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_15_Figure_3.jpeg)

*Figure 5. A button to choose a file*

After downloading the file, check the correctness of the specified data, then print and sign the document by hand.

An electronic version of the signed document shall be attached to the request via upload form (open the form by clicking the **[ Choose file ]** button).

A hard copy of the signed document shall be sent by mail to the address specified in the message from the technical support service. The message will be sent to email specified in the **"Email "** field in the block **"1. Contact person"**.

After completion of the registration and successful authorization in the user account of the Partners and Integrators Register, the uploaded file will be available for viewing in the section **"My Organization" › "Documents"**.

## <span id="page-19-1"></span>**Organization card interface**

To go to an organization card, select a **"My organization"** section in the **"Main menu"**.

• organization's identification number in the Partners and Integrators Register (element 1);

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_19_Figure_14.jpeg)

*Figure 10. Organization card interface elements*

The organization name can be edited by means of the icon only until the organization profile is in the **"Active"** status.

- request status (element 4);
- registration date and time (element 5);
- last name, first name and patronymic of the primary contact of the organization (element 6);
- organization type (element 7). For more information on principle of assignment of the organization type, see section ["Table of correspondence of organization types to activity types"](#page-11-0);
- information on documents binding on the organization type (element 8). For more information, see section "[Table of correspondence between document mandatory and organization types"](#page-92-0);
- profile control tabs (element 9).

The **"Organization"** tab contains registration details, some of which are editable. For more information, see ["Editing organization data](#page-20-0)";

In the **"Organization"** tab, a service provider can obtain an individual identifier (UUID) in the **"UUID for SUZ"** field, which can be used to work in the OMS.

**ATTENTION**

The individual identifier (UUID) to work in the OMS is displayed in the **"UUID for SUZ"** field only after [Soft and Hardware Package Contract](#page-101-0) is signed by the Operator

### <span id="page-20-0"></span>**Editing organization data**

To introduce changes to the registration data, go to **"My organization" › "Organization"** section in

the **"Main menu"** and click button in the organization card.

![](Инструкция_по_работе_с_РИ_en-v32.0-08.05.2026-at-08-52-29_images/_page_21_Figure_1.jpeg)

*Figure 12. A button to edit a profile in the organization card*

The following fields are available for modification in this mode:

- **"KPP"**;
- **"Abbreviated name"** (the value will be blocked from changes after an organization profile is in the **"Active"** status);
- **"Long name"** (the value will be blocked from changes after an organization profile is in the **"Active"** status);
- **"Company's main email"**;
- **"Telephone"**;
- **"Website"**;
- **"Profile"**;
- **"Presence of partner network"** if applicable, activate by moving the switch in the (on) mode;
- **"Number of partners in partner network"** specify a value by means of direct input if **"Presence of partner network"** value is active;
- **"Full name of director"** specify last name, first name and patronymic of the head of the organization (in line with EGRUL).

The value will be blocked from changes after the organization profile is in the **"Active"** status. The exceptions are organizations, which have the only organization type - **"Goods circulation participant"**. A possibility to change the **"Full name of director"** field will be not blocked for these organizations.

• **"Full name of attorney-in-fact"**.

If it is filled in, sign a power of attorney in the **"Documents"** section, after the corresponding document is added by the Operator.

### <span id="page-25-0"></span>**Management of integration and partner products**

The Partners and Integrators Register provides two product types:

• partner products.

The partner products include products, which do not interact with OMS API, e.g. online cash registers, UTD Operators, portable data terminal barcode scanners, etc. Such products do not pass acceptance tests on interaction with TT GIS or MDLP FGIS components, while signing of such documents as the Declaration, NDA and Application to enter into a Memorandum is mandatory for them.

More information on partner products can be found in the section ["Partner solutions](#page-84-0)".

• integration products.

Integration products include software, which is intended to interact with OMS API and should be tested by the Operator in accordance with commissioning check procedure adopted for the OMS API. Software versions that have passed the acceptance tests, receive a **"Admitted to the production environment"** status, as well as a unique registration key to work on the production environment of the TT GIS or MDLP FGIS. Access to such software appears in the OMS user interface in the **"Registry of integration solutions"** section and becomes available to all goods circulation participants (with appropriate visibility settings).

It is also mandatory to sign such documents as the Declaration, NDA and Application to enter into a Memorandum.

For more information on integration products, see section "[Integration products](#page-26-0)". For more information on statistics of integration products usage, see section "[Viewing statistics of integration](#page-104-0) [products usage"](#page-104-0).
